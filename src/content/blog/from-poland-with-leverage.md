Working independently changes the shape of delivery.

There are fewer handoffs, fewer incentives to hide risk, and less pressure to maintain process theater. That matters when the goal is not just to produce code, but to improve the speed and quality of decisions around the code.

Operating from Poland gives G2Labs a practical advantage: strong overlap with European teams, predictable communication windows, and access to a mature engineering market without the cost structure of a large agency.

That is the leverage model: small surface area, high signal, and work that compounds after the engagement ends.
