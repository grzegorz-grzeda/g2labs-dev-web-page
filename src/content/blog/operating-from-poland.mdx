Running a small web company from Poland gives G2Labs a useful constraint set.

It encourages pragmatic tooling, async communication, and projects that can be shipped cleanly without large operational overhead.

For this site, that means a static architecture, a lightweight publishing model, and hosting that is easy to reason about.

Those choices reduce cost, reduce maintenance, and keep the surface area small enough for one person to manage well.
